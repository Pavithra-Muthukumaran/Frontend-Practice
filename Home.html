About

Arunkumar Manoharan is a civil engineer, academic and researcher with significant contributions to the field of civil engineering. He obtained his Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from K.S. Rangasamy College of Technology, Namakkal and his Master's degree in Structural Engineering from Kumaraguru College of Technology, Coimbatore. With ten years of experience as an Assistant Professor at Kongu Engineering College, Erode, he contributed as the NBA Criteria 3 Coordinator at the departmental level and was a core team member of the NAAC Criteria 1 team at the institutional level. Throughout his academic career, he has engaged in various testing and consultancy projects, including stability assessments, third-party road inspections, and mix design. He has published research papers in international journals and participated in various national and international conferences. He has now transitioned to a full-time research scholar role at BTCM division - IIT Madras, under the scheme of IITM DU JDP, where his research focuses on waste in applying Self-Compacting Concrete to promote environmental sustainability and circular economy.
